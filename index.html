About

Polyvalent and rigorous, Bruno has 8 years of experience in the European power sector. He has gained experience across all the power supply chain from generation to supply, working for companies such as RTE (French TSO), EDF Energy, Limejump and Reactive Technologies. With a wide experience ranging from economics, strategy, project management and operations, Bruno has advised start-ups, PE funds and utilities on topics varying from PPA contracts to battery storage market studies.

Bruno has acquired in depth knowledge of power markets and power sector regulation, and also benefits from a strong network in the French energy sector especially in Policy&Regulation (CRE, Enedis, RTE). Interested in start-up, Bruno has recently looked in depth at different venture ideas.
