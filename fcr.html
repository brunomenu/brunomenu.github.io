You'll find on this page some graphs on the prices for Frequency Containment Reserve (FCR) in France, Belgium, Netherlands, Germany and Switzerland. The graphs are interactive so you can zoom in/out and choose which country you want to display.

FCR prices since creation

The graph below displays the weekly FCR prices since the creation of FCR. It shows the weekly average of prices since FCR moved to daily auctions in July 2019. By default the graph shows the "all" curve which is the weighted average price. You can display price per individual country by clicking on the country name in the legend.

FCR prices since move to daily procurement in July 2019

The graph below displays the FCR prices since the move to daily procurement. By default the French price is displayed but you can display additional countries by clicking on the country name in the legend.
